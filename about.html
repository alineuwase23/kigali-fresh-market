About Kigali Fresh Market

We are Rwanda's trusted online food store, bringing fresh and authentic products directly to your door.

Our Story

Kigali Fresh Market was founded with a simple but powerful vision — to make fresh, quality Rwandan food products accessible to everyone through the power of technology.

We started as a small initiative to support local farmers and food producers in Rwanda. Today, we have grown into a trusted online marketplace serving hundreds of customers across Kigali.

Every product we sell is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality, freshness, and authenticity. We believe in supporting local agriculture while delivering convenience to our customers.

Meet Our Founder

The person behind the vision

Aline Uwase

👑 Founder & CEO

Aline Uwase is the founder and CEO of Kigali Fresh Market. With a passion for technology and a deep love for Rwandan culture and food, she created this platform to bridge the gap between local farmers and modern consumers.

As a student of Information Systems Management at UNILAK, Aline combined her technical skills with entrepreneurial spirit to build a platform that makes a real difference in people's lives.

"I believe technology can transform how we access food in Rwanda. My goal is to make fresh, quality products available to every household with just a few clicks."
